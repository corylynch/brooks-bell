The Six Pillars of a Successful Experimentation Program

Team

Experiementation requires a complex mix of people from across your organization. Brooks Bell can help you transform a group of individuals into a team focused on strengthening your experimentation ecosystem.

Tech and Tools

We can help you understand, implement, and manage analytic platforms, tag management systems, databases, mouse tracking, surveys, and user testing technologies so you’re positioned for growth.

Culture

Without a company culture that embraces experiementation, you can’t have a successful program. Experimentation requires enthusiastic advocates from every level of the organization before you can transform the company norm. Our Experts can help you earn that buy-in by defining piorities, encouraging participation, reporting strong results and communicating the value of the program.

Performance

Our team can help you gauge and improve performance through program measures including volume, velocity, win rate, learn rate, bust rate and custom measurements based on your objectives.

Strategy

Brooks Bell has vast experience in creating successful experimentation strategies that utilize date, answer critical questions, solve key problems and advance your organization’s solutions. Brooks Bell’s unique methodology uses behavioral economics to fuel user-centric, performance-driven experiments that can take your organization to the next level.

Process

From charting tasks and defining roles and responsibilities to creative QA checklist and establishing implementation guidelines, we’re your partner for ensuring consistency, reducing risk, and increasing reliability of results.
